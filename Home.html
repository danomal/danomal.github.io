About Us

100+ Women Who Care--Naperville, began in February, 2009 with 29 members. The goal was to reach a membership of 100, which was reached and surpassed in the spring of 2013. Today the membership numbers over 140, more than four times what it was at that first meeting in 2009. Since we first met in 2009, we have donated $715,941 to local charities.
